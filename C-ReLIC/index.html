Decomposing the Latent Space:
A Phase-aware Plug-and-Play Approach for Universal Compressed Image Restoration

Di You, Haotian Wu, and Pier Luigi Dragotti
Imperial College London, Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering
Paper Code

Abstract

In this work, we propose a phase-aware and plug-and-play framework for universal compressed image restoration, termed C-ReLIC (Compression Restoration with Latent Invertible Consistency). At its core is Bidirectional Latent-Operator Consistency (Bi-LOC), a novel mechanism that factorizes latent features into measurement and detail components via an invertible operator, enabling bidirectional consistency for both fidelity and detail preservation. To further enhance quality, Bi-LOC operates within a phase-aware strategy that anchors early diffusion to global structure and refines perceptual quality in later stages. Extensive experiments show that C-ReLIC achieves state-of-the-art across diverse codecs, offering a superior fidelity and perception trade-off.
